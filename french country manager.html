1. Alpha Early Deals, a product enabling everyone to invest like a VC.

Alpha Early Deals offers SwissBorg app users exclusive access to projects launching their tokens before public sale, as well as investments in selected tokens of existing projects curated by our investment team. Unlike a traditional token launch platform (Launchpad), Alpha stands out for its accessibility to all SwissBorg users, whether they are premium members enjoying enhanced services or standard members. The Deals are structured across four distinct vaults: - A vault dedicated to premium users, offering them a significant share of opportunities. - Another reserved for Top BORG Holders, rewarding substantial holders of BORG. - Two public vaults, one in BORG and the other in ETH or USDC, accessible to all app users.


Our approach differs from traditional venture capital investments or typical launchpads. Specifically, we eliminate the need for high initial capital, specialized expertise, and any form of token lottery or guarantee. This approach aims to ensure fairness in participation and enables our entire community to benefit from investment opportunities, emphasizing user engagement and responsiveness..

2. Nestborg, the new SwissBorg's crypto incubator !

To empower you to achieve your dreams just as SwissBorg did in 2017, we've created Nestborg. Nestborg is a next-generation crypto incubator offering an innovative and engaging approach to attract the hottest projects to join us! We've designed a comprehensive and inclusive program specifically crafted to help you complete your fundraising as swiftly as startup Aethir, securing the necessary funds to develop your idea into a finished product! This program includes the opportunity to secure a portion of your funding through grants from blockchain foundations. These grants are non-dilutive, meaning you don't need to give up equity in your company to obtain crucial initial funding. We support you throughout the process by assisting in preparing and refining your pitch and project description with the help of a dedicated consultant. Our goal is to maximize your chances of success right from the start. We understand that grants alone may not suffice to cover all your financing needs. That's why we also offer you access to our Alpha Early Deals product. This product allows you to meet your fundraising goals using SwissBorg as a reliable and effective solution. By collaborating closely throughout your development journey, we provide continuous support through our dedicated consultant and our additional program, Meta Exchange. Furthermore, to recognize your loyalty, we plan to gradually reduce fees for your future fundraising rounds on our platform, from seed phase to Series A. So, if you are a company with a seasoned and complementary founding team capable of efficiently overcoming the challenges of project development, and you have a project that brings significant innovation or marked improvement over existing market solutions, targets sufficiently large addressable markets with substantial growth potential, and includes distinctive technological innovations such as patented technologies, while also identifying risks associated with project execution, including technical and regulatory challenges through careful risk assessment and effective mitigation strategies. Then join us at SwissBorg to benefit from a platform that truly supports your growth in the blockchain ecosystem. Together, we can realize your ambitions and propel your project toward success.

"Oh, and by the way, this month, SwissBorg's investment team has validated the 6 projects that our alpha hunters have informed us about! (memorandum here) The first 3 on the list will be integrated into #SwissBorgAlpha, and the next 3 will be integrated into #SwissBorgMEX."

  • Nefture a Web3 platform specializing in real-time security and risk prevention that detects vulnerabilities on-chain and protects digital assets, protocols, and asset managers from losses or significant threats through its monitoring tools. Key services include real-time cryptographic transaction security and a threat monitoring platform offering precise exploit detection and fully customized alerts covering hundreds of risk types, with clear expertise in DeFi. Today, Nefture collaborates with leading projects and asset managers, providing unparalleled security solutions that mitigate threats and ensure the safety of their portfolios and transactions. Nefture's comprehensive security solutions, innovative tools, and strategic partnerships position them as a key player in the blockchain security domain. Their commitment to protecting digital assets and ensuring compliance, coupled with active involvement in the crypto community, underscores their leadership and growth potential.
  • Inflowpay an innovative payment application that merges blockchain technology with traditional payment networks to facilitate faster, secure, and cost-effective transactions. It enables businesses to conduct direct bank-to-bank payments without intermediaries, reducing transaction fees and eliminating risks of fraud or chargebacks. Additionally, seamless integrations with existing systems and merchants' full control over funds via self-custodial wallets make this service highly appealing and revolutionary for modern commerce.
    SwissBorg could find a partnership with InFlowPay compelling as it would enable their Smart Send service to extend beyond the current SwissBorg user community. InFlowPay facilitates transactions to external users at very low costs, thereby expanding the reach of Smart Send. This integration could attract more users to SwissBorg by making payments to external accounts as simple and economical as current internal transactions, enhancing the convenience and adoption of the service.
  • Byzantine finance a decentralized and secure restaking platform that offers scalable infrastructure for exchanges, wallets, custodians, and product issuers. It utilizes distributed validation technologies like EigenLayer, enhancing security and enabling quick integration with minimal coding. The platform supports AVS wallets and ensures protection against missed rewards. Users can configure their own validation parameters based on specific needs while benefiting from the platform's optimal protections and performance. By facilitating access to simple APIs and SDKs, Byzantine Finance ensures optimal liquidity and promotes cross-chain and cross-protocol interoperability. Currently operational on the testnet with a significant locked value, Byzantine Finance represents an innovative and promising solution in the decentralized finance space, accelerating the go-to-market for restaking products.
  • Mina is designed to address the issues of size and complexity found in traditional blockchains. Unlike other blockchains, which are often bulky, Mina remains consistently lightweight, at about 22 kilobytes, allowing any device, even a smartphone, to become a full node. By utilizing zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs), Mina ensures private and secure transactions while maintaining a minimal chain size. Its small size enables increased decentralization, enhancing network security and resilience. Additionally, Mina can interact with other blockchains and systems, offering exceptional scalability and interoperability.
  • Akash Network a decentralized alternative to centralized cloud services such as AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure. It enables developers to rent unused computing capacity from global servers, thereby reducing costs and increasing efficiency.Costs are significantly reduced, which appeals to startups and SMEs. The flexibility allows for easy adjustment of computing capacities. The blockchain ensures security and transparency of transactions, while an open market promotes competition. The growth of the cloud market, adoption of decentralized technologies, and focus on innovative solutions are favorable trends for Akash Network. Its experienced team and strategic partnerships enhance its credibility.
  • StarkNet a second-layer scaling solution for Ethereum, utilizing zk-Rollup to enhance transaction speed and reduce costs. It processes transactions off-chain, thereby lowering fees and increasing speed, while ensuring security through zk-Rollup technology. StarkNet enables broader adoption of decentralized applications (dApps) and decentralized exchanges (DEX) by making transactions more accessible and efficient. By leveraging the Ethereum ecosystem, StarkNet maintains decentralization and security while offering necessary scalability.
